Homecoming: October 19, 2013

Alumni Corporation Meeting: 10:00am

The meeting will be held on campus in the Salisbury-Young room of the Johnson Student Center. The meeting will include:

  • Voting on the new Alumni Corporation board members
  • Voting on the recipient of the Excellent New Member Scholarship
  • General discussion about a few important issues such as fundraising and housing

Brunch: 11:00am

Brunch will be held at Coffee Table in Eagle Rock at 11:00am. If you are interested in making a donation towards the brunch, please do so below. Donations are not required, but highly encouraged!
